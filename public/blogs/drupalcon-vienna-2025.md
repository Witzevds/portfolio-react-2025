# DrupalCon Vienna 2025 Highlights

Attending DrupalCon Vienna 2025 was a milestone moment in my career. Having my Figma-to-Drupal AI workflow featured during Dries Buytaert's keynote was an incredible honor.

## The Journey

What started as an internship project at Calibrate turned into something that caught the attention of the Drupal founder himself. The journey from initial rejection to keynote feature taught me invaluable lessons about persistence and innovation.

## The Keynote Moment

Watching Dries present my work on the main stage was surreal. The workflow I developed addresses a real pain point in the Drupal ecosystem:

- **Problem**: Manual conversion of design files to Drupal components is time-consuming
- **Solution**: AI-powered automation that maintains design fidelity
- **Impact**: Reduces development time by up to 70%
